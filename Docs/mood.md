# Wedding Invitation Design Mood

This is NOT a wedding template.

This is an interactive website telling the story of two engineers.

The experience should feel handcrafted, calm and timeless.

Avoid:
- Generic wedding templates
- Excessive animations
- Pink romantic aesthetics
- Floral overload
- Heavy gradients
- Glassmorphism

Prefer:
- Editorial layouts
- Large photography
- Generous whitespace
- Vintage paper
- Fine typography
- Subtle interaction
